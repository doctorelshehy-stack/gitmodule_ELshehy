Imaging Investigations

1. Endoscopy (Gold Standard)

  • Identifies exact bleeding source
  • Allows therapeutic intervention
  • Forrest classification for risk stratification

2. Chest & Abdominal X-ray

  • Rule out perforation
  • Assess for aspiration

3. Angiography

  • For obscure / refractory bleeding
  • Can be therapeutic (embolization)
