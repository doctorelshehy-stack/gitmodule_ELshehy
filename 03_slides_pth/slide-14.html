Gastric Varices & Rescue Therapies

Gastric Varices Management

Why not banding?

  • Adequate suction of gastric varix into ligator usually impossible
  • Thicker gastric wall → bands fall off prematurely
  • Rebleeding more frequent

Treatment of Choice: Histoacryl Injection

  • Histoacryl: non-toxic acrylic blue dye — potent adhesive on contact with blood
  • Adequate dilution with Lipiodol (1:1 ratio) is crucial
  • Obliterates the varix lumen

Rescue Therapies

Balloon Tamponade (Sengstaken-Blakemore)

  • For 5–10% uncontrolled by endoscopy + pharmacotherapy
  • Hemostasis in 60–90%
  • Temporary bridge until TIPS or surgery

TIPS (Transjugular Intrahepatic Portosystemic Shunt)

  • Hemostasis in up to 95% of acute cases
  • Lower rebleeding rate
  • Risk: deterioration of hepatic function / hepatic coma

Surgical Treatment

  • Distal spleno-renal shunt
  • Reserved for refractory cases
