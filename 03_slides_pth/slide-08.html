UGIB — Symptoms & Signs

Symptoms

  • Fatigue, palpitation, SOB (anemia)
  • Lightheadedness / syncope
  • Epigastric pain, heartburn
  • Diffuse abdominal pain

Key Terms

Term Description
Hematemesis Vomiting blood — coffee-ground (slow) or fresh (rapid)
Melena Tarry, offensive, semi-solid stool — blood in GI >14 hrs
Hematochezia Bright red blood per rectum — usually LGIB, but may be massive UGIB

Signs on Examination

General / Vital Signs

  • Signs of shock: cold extremities, tachycardia, hypotension
  • Chest pain, confusion, delirium, oliguria

Skin Features of Cirrhosis

  • Palmar erythema, spider nevi
  • Bleeding disorders: purpura, ecchymosis

Other Signs

  • Dehydration: dry mucosa, sunken eyes
  • Tumors: nodular liver, abdominal mass, lymphadenopathy
  • Comorbid disease findings
