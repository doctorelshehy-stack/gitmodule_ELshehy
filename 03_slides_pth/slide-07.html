UGIB — Causes

Esophageal Causes

  • Esophageal varices
  • Esophagitis
  • Esophageal ulcer
  • Mallory-Weiss tear
  • Esophageal carcinoma

Gastric Causes

  • Gastric ulcer
  • Gastritis
  • Gastric cancer
  • Gastric varices
  • GAVE
  • Portal hypertensive gastropathy
  • Dieulafoy's lesion

Duodenal Causes

  • Duodenal ulcer
  • Vascular malformation
  • Aorto-enteric fistula
  • Hemobilia
  • Hemosuccus pancreaticus
  • SMA syndrome

Key: Esophageal variceal bleeding and bleeding from peptic ulcer are the two most common causes of UGIB.
