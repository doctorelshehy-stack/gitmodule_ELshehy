IBS — Pharmacological Management

Pain & Spasm

  • Anticholinergics: dicyclomine, hyoscyamine
  • Antispasmodics: peppermint oil, pinaverium, trimebutine, cimetropium/dicyclomine
  • TCAs: imipramine, amitriptyline

Diarrhea

  • Antidiarrheals: diphenoxylate, loperamide
  • Serotonin receptor antagonists: alosetron
  • Rifaximin: especially in diarrhea-predominant

Constipation

  • Bulk-forming laxatives
  • Chloride channel activators: lubiprostone
  • GC-C agonists: linaclotide

Motility & Microbiome

  • Prokinetics
  • Probiotics & prebiotics: live/attenuated bacteria with health benefits
  • Bifidobacterium infantis 35624 — beneficial effect, normalizes anti/pro-inflammatory cytokine ratio
