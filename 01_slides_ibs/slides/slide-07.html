IBS — Investigations

Screening Studies

  • CBC with differential: screen for anemia, inflammation, infection
  • Comprehensive metabolic panel: metabolic disorders, dehydration/electrolyte abnormalities
  • Stool examinations: ova & parasites, enteric pathogens, leukocytes, C. difficile toxin, Giardia antigen

History-Specific Studies

  • Hydrogen breath testing: bacterial overgrowth, lactose/fructose intolerance
  • tTG antibody + small bowel biopsy in IBS-D → celiac disease
  • Thyroid function tests
  • Serum calcium: hyperparathyroidism
  • ESR & CRP: nonspecific inflammation screening
