Candida Esophagitis

Endoscopic Findings
• White plaques on erythematous mucosa
• Whitish-yellow plaques without visible ulceration
Histology
• Pseudohyphae and budding spores
• Embedded in necrotic debris
Most Common
• Most common cause of infectious esophagitis
Treatment
• Nystatin swish and swallow
• Ketoconazole
• Fluconazole
