Dysphagia

Definition
• Difficulty swallowing
Remember:
• Dysphagia = Difficulty in swallowing
• Odynophagia = Pain on swallowing
Key Questions in Dysphagia
1) Difficulty in starting swallowing?
2) Associated symptoms? (regurgitation, change in voice pitch, weight loss)
3) Solids, liquids, or both?
4) Intermittent or progressive?
5) History of heartburn?
6) Change in eating habits/diet?
