GERD — Overview

GERD — Signs & Symptoms
Non-esophageal
Respiratory:
• Chronic cough
• Wheezing
• Aspiration pneumonia
Non-respiratory:
• Sore throat
• Hoarseness
• Dental erosions
Esophageal
Typical:
• Heartburn
• Acid regurgitation
Atypical:
• Chest pain
• Dysphagia (late)
• Odynophagia (rare)
GERD — Gastroscopy-Guided Management
NERD
Non-erosive reflux disease
• Normal esophagus on endoscopy
• Aim: symptom relief only
→ PPI PRN
Esophagitis
Esophageal inflammation
• Inflamed mucosa on endoscopy
• Aim: heal inflammation
→ PPI indefinitely
→ or Surgical fundoplication
