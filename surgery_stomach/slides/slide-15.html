Carcinoma — Staging & Spread

AJCC TNM Classification
Primary Tumor
Tis: Carcinoma in situ
T1: Lamina propria/submucosa
T2: Muscularis propria/subserosa
T3: Penetrates serosa
T4: Invades adjacent structures
Lymph Nodes
N0: No metastasis
N1: 1-6 regional nodes
N2: 7-15 regional nodes
N3: >15 regional nodes
Spread Patterns
• Direct: through muscularis, serosa → adjacent organs
• Lymphatic: permeation and emboli (Troisier's sign)
• Blood-borne: liver → lung, bone (uncommon without nodes)
• Trans-peritoneal: ascites, Krukenberg's tumor
• Sister Joseph's nodule (umbilicus metastasis)
