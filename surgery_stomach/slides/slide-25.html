Sleeve Gastrectomy

Procedure
• The standard operation nowadays
• Lesser curve-based gastric tube constructed
• Over size 32-36 Fr bougie
Mechanism
• Initially thought to be purely restrictive
• Studies suggest gastric emptying is accelerated
• Not delayed as previously thought
Key Point:
Sleeve gastrectomy is now the most commonly performed bariatric procedure worldwide due to its simplicity, effectiveness, and lower complication rates compared to gastric bypass.
