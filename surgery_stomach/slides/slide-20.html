Post-Gastrectomy Complications

Operative Complications
• Intraoperative bleeding
• Organ injury (spleen, pancreas, diaphragm)
• Atelectasis, pneumonia, respiratory failure
• DVT, Pulmonary Embolism
• Wound infection, subphrenic abscess
• Acute Pancreatitis
Early Post-op
• Anastomotic hemorrhage or leakage
• Duodenal stump leak
• Small bowel obstruction
• Stomal obstruction
Late Post-op
• Anastomotic stricture
• Marginal ulcer bleeding
• Gastro-gastric fistula
• Post-gastrectomy syndrome (dumping)
• Small stomach syndrome
• Remnant carcinoma
