Carcinoma — Clinical Features

Early vs Advanced
• Early gastric cancer: no specific features (mimics benign dyspepsia)
• Advanced: features usually obvious
Symptoms & Signs
Early Symptoms
• Vague discomfort (difficult to distinguish from dyspepsia)
• Anorexia, meat aversion
• Pronounced weight loss
• Early satiety, bloating
Late Symptoms
• Vomiting, distension
• Tumor bleeds → iron-deficiency anemia
• Epigastric mass
• Hematemesis (coffee ground, seldom severe)
Metastatic Signs
• Virchow node (left supraclavicular)
• Blumer shelf (rectal)
• Trousseau's sign (thrombophlebitis)
• Deep venous thrombosis
Troisier's sign
• Supraclavicular lymphadenopathy
• From submucosal lymphatic spread
• Indicates advanced disease
