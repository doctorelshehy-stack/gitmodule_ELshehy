Bariatric Surgery — Overview

Advantages
• Long-term survival benefit
• Improves obesity-related disease
• Improves quality of life
• Effectively reduces cancer
• Controls metabolic syndrome:
— Type II diabetes, hypertension
— Dyslipidemia, PCOS
Indications
• BMI ≥ 40
• BMI ≥ 35 + type II DM (onset <10 years)
• BMI 30-34.9 + type II DM (onset <10 years)
Classification
Restrictive
• Decrease stomach size
• Limit food intake
• Promote satiety
Sleeve gastrectomy
Malabsorptive
• Reduce intestinal length
• Reduce calorie absorption
Jejuno-ileal bypass
Combined
• Both restriction + malabsorption
Gastric bypass
Bilio-pancreatic diversion
