Carcinoma — Treatment

Resectable Tumors
• Early gastric cancer
• Advanced confined to stomach + perigastric nodes
• Treatment: Gastrectomy + lymphadenectomy
+/- adjuvant chemotherapy/radiotherapy
Types of Gastrectomy
• Subtotal gastrectomy
• Total gastrectomy
• Distal gastrectomy
• Proximal gastrectomy
Irresectable Tumors
• Distant metastasis
• Treatment: Chemotherapy +/- radiotherapy
• Palliative surgery only for:
— Obstruction
— Bleeding
