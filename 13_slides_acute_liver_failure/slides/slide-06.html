Complications — Cardiorespiratory

Cardiovascular

  • Hypotension and circulatory instability are common in ALF
  • Patients are often hypovolaemic at presentation
  • If hypotension persists after adequate volume resuscitation → vasopressors
  • Functional adrenocortical insufficiency can occur
  • IV glucocorticoids considered if unresponsive to fluid & vasopressors

Respiratory

  • With early intubation, respiratory failure is unusual initially
  • May develop later from nosocomial infection

Management Priorities

  • Early recognition in critical care setting
  • Early contact with specialist liver centre
  • Identify & treat precipitants
  • Optimise conditions for liver regeneration
  • Continuous prognostic assessment for LT waitlisting
