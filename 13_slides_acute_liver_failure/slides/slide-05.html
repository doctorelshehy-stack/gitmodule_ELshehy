Investigations in Acute Liver Failure

ABGs & Haematology

  • ABGs: include lactate and arterial ammonia
  • FBC and coagulation studies (INR, PT, fibrinogen)
  • Blood group

Biochemistry

  • LFTs: AST, ALT, ALP, GGT, bilirubin, albumin
  • Renal function & electrolytes (Na, K, urea, creatinine, Mg, PO4)
  • Glucose, amylase, TFTs, CK, lipids

Hepatitis Serology & Viral Screen

  • Anti-HAV IgM, HBsAg, anti-HB core IgM, anti-HEV IgM
  • EBV, CMV, HSV, HIV, adenovirus, parvovirus

Autoantibodies & Toxicology

  • ANA, anti-smooth muscle actin, anti-LKM antibodies, immunoglobulins
  • Paracetamol and salicylate levels
  • Serum copper and caeruloplasmin (Wilson's screen)

Imaging

Liver ultrasound: assess patency of hepatic & portal veins and hepatic artery
