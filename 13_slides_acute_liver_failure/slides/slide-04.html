Etiology of Acute Liver Failure

1. Acute Viral Infections

  • Hepatitis A, B, E — most common in developing world
  • Rare: herpes simplex, CMV, EBV, adenovirus, parvoviruses

2. Drug-Induced Liver Injury

Major cause in developed countries. Paracetamol is the most common drug cause.

3. Ischaemic Liver Injury

Critically ill with circulatory failure or septic shock → significant hepatic necrosis.

4. Metabolic Diseases

  • Wilson's disease
  • Malignant infiltration
  • Budd-Chiari syndrome
  • Heatshock

5. Pregnancy-Related

  • Acute fatty liver of pregnancy
  • HELLP syndrome (Haemolysis, Elevated Liver enzymes, Low Platelets)
