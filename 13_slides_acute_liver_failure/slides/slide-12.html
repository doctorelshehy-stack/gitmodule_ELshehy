Alternative Therapies

Rationale

  • Limited availability of LT
  • Need to stabilise urgently waitlisted patients
  • Led to interest in artificial liver support devices

MARS

  • Molecular Adsorbent Recirculating System
  • Uses albumin dialysis to extract toxins normally processed by the liver
  • Some case series suggested clinical & biochemical improvements

MARS Evidence

Multicentre RCT of MARS vs conventional management in ALF did not demonstrate any survival benefit.

High-Volume Plasma Exchange

Initial reports suggest may be beneficial in selected patients.

Summary

ALF remains a medical emergency with high mortality. Liver transplantation is the definitive treatment. NAC is the most important specific therapy. King's College Criteria guide transplant listing.
