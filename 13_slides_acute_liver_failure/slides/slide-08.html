Complications — Metabolic

Hypoglycaemia

  • Patients with ALF are at high risk
  • Preventable with IV glucose infusion

Fluid Management

  • Large volume hypotonic fluid infusion is avoided
  • Risk of hyponatraemia

Nutritional Support

  • Essential in highly catabolic patients
  • Monitor blood ammonia concentration
  • Transient reduction in protein load if necessary

Coagulopathy

Despite markedly abnormal standard coagulation tests, spontaneous bleeding is very uncommon.
