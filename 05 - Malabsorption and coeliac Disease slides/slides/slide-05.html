Nutritional Manifestations of Malabsorption

Anemia

Blood loss or malabsorption of iron, B12 or folic acid

Edema

Hypoproteinemia from protein malabsorption or protein-losing enteropathy

Cholesterol GB Stones

Bile acid malabsorption → supersaturated bile → stones

Osteomalacia

Malabsorption of Calcium and Vitamin D

Vitamin A Deficiency

Night blindness

Extra-Intestinal Manifestations (IBD)

Joint Complications (~20% of IBD patients)

  • Axial (central) arthritis
  • Peripheral arthritis

Ophthalmologic (3-4% of IBD)

  • Episcleritis — burning eyes, scleral injection
  • Parallels disease course
  • Resolves with IBD treatment + topical steroids
