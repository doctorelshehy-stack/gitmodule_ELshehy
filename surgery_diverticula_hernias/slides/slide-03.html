Diverticula — Definition & Classification

Definition
A blind loop pouch which is continuous with the lumen of the gastrointestinal tract
By Location
• Esophageal: Upper (Zenker), Middle (tracheal bifurcation), Lower (epiphrenic)
• Small Bowel: Duodenal, Jejunal, Meckel (ileal)
• Colonic: Diverticulosis (sigmoid most common)
By Histology
• True: ALL layers of the wall protrude
• False: Only mucosa + submucosa bulge through weak points in muscularis propria
• Zenker = usually FALSE
• Traction = usually TRUE
By Pathophysiology
Pulsion:
• Inadequate sphincter relaxation + ↑ intraluminal pressure → outpouching
Traction:
• Mediastinal inflammation + scarring → retraction of wall
