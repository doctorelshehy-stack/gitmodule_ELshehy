Strangulated Hernia — Treatment
Urgent surgical interference!
Pre-op: NG tube, IV fluids, analgesia, antibiotics
Operative Principles:
1. Open sac fundus first → evacuate toxic fluid
2. Divide constricting ring
3. Pull out contents → assess viability
4. Gangrenous content → excise (small bowel/omentum)
5. Colonic gangrene → diverting colostomy
6. Suspicious bowel → warm packs + high O₂ + re-evaluate (peristalsis matters more than color)
7. Hernioplasty unless contaminated → herniorrhaphy
