Anatomical Types (Indirect)
Congenital:
• Whole processus vaginalis present
• Reaches bottom of scrotum
• Testis within sac content
Infantile:
• Tunica vaginalis extends to external ring
Adult:
• Bubonocele: limited to inguinal canal
• Funicular: reaches neck of scrotum
• Complete: reaches bottom of scrotum
Sliding Hernia:
• Viscus forms part of sac wall
• Common: bladder, cecum, sigmoid
• Do NOT dissect sliding organ
• Invaginate whole sac
