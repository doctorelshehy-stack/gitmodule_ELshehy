HCC — Pathology

Gross Appearance
Massive Form
• Localized mass
• Fibrolamellar HCC: young females
— Better prognosis
Nodular Form
• Multiple nodules scattered
Diffuse Form
• Diffuse infiltration throughout liver
Microscopic Types
HCC
• Malignant hepatocytes
• Little stroma, high vascularity
• Derived from hepatic artery
Cholangiocarcinoma
• Adenocarcinoma of bile duct epithelium
• Usually late presentation
• Poor prognosis
Other Types
• Hepato-cholangiocarcinoma (mixed)
• Hepatoblastoma (children)
