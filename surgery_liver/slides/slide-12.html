LT — Evaluation & Contraindications

Pre-Transplant Evaluation
Laboratory
• Viral hepatitis, HIV testing
• PSA assay, TB test
• Liver and renal function
Imaging
• Chest X-ray, PFT, ABG
• ECG, Stress echo, MRI
• Liver angiography
Procedures
• EGD + Colonoscopy
• Mammogram, Cervical screening
• Dental evaluation
Vaccination
• Hep A, B, Pneumococcus, H. influenzae, Influenza
• Psychiatric & social assessment
Absolute Contraindications
• Active uncontrolled bacterial/fungal infection
• Active non-hepatobiliary malignancy
• Advanced cardiopulmonary disease
• Alcohol or drug abuse
Relative Contraindications
• Age >70 years
• Portal vein thrombosis
• Pulmonary hypertension
• Severe hypoxemia (non-HPS)
• Congenital cardiac anomalies
• Noncompliance with medical care
• Psychiatric conditions
• HIV infection
• HCC (beyond Milan criteria)
• Cholangiocarcinoma
