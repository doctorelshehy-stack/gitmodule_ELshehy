OJ — Treatment

Preoperative Preparation
Correct Clotting Dysfunction
• IV Vit K + Fresh blood transfusion
Guard Against Liver Failure
• High glucose intake
• Broad-spectrum antibiotics (if cholangitis)
Guard Against Renal Failure
• IV fluids, IV mannitol, oral bile salts
Calculi — ERCP
• Endoscopic sphincterotomy
• Balloon extraction of CBD stone
• Then laparoscopic cholecystectomy
Malignant — Whipple Procedure
• For cancer head of pancreas
• Resection of: distal CBD + head of pancreas + duodenum
• Triple anastomosis:
— Choledocho-jejunostomy
— Gastro-jejunostomy
— Pancreatico-jejunostomy
Only ~20% resectable at presentation
Palliative Options
1) Endoscopic Biliary Stenting
• Plastic or self-expanding metal stent
2) Biliary Bypass Surgery
• Roux-en-Y hepaticojejunostomy
• ± concomitant gastrojejunostomy
3) Percutaneous Biliary Drainage
• When ERCP fails
