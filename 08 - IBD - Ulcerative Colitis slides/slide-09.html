Complications

Similar to Crohn's Disease, except:

Liver Problems

  • More common than in CD
  • Especially PSC in men

Colorectal Cancer

  • Greater risk than in CD
  • Risk ↑ with duration & extent

Toxic Megacolon

  • Transverse colon >6 cm on AXR
  • Immediate danger of perforation

CANCER RISK TIMELINE

Pancolitis: 5% at 10 yr, 15% at 20 yr. Overall relative risk is 8%. Risk also ↑ with active mucosal inflammation and sclerosing cholangitis.
