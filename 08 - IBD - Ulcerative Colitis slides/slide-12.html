Key Takeaways

UC is an inflammatory disease of colonic mucosa — rectum always involved

Rectal bleeding is the hallmark; non-bloody diarrhea is the initial presentation

5-ASA is the mainstay for mild-moderate; steroids for acute flares

Watch for PSC, colorectal cancer risk, and toxic megacolon

Colectomy is curative — 20-25% eventually need surgery; normal life expectancy
