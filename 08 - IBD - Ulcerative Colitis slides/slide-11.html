Surgical Treatment

Colectomy is CURATIVE — Aim for colectomy with ileal pouch-anal anastomosis (IPAA)

INDICATIONS FOR SURGERY

  • Failure of adequate medical therapy
  • Toxic megacolon
  • Uncontrollable bleeding
  • Pre-cancerous changes (dysplasia)
  • Inability to taper corticosteroids
  • Overt malignancy

COLECTOMY RATES

1%

After 1st year (all patients)

20-25%

Eventually undergo colectomy

Screening: Regular colonoscopy and biopsy in pancolitis of ≥8 years is indicated
