Investigations

Sigmoidoscopy

  • With mucosal biopsy
  • Often sufficient for diagnosis
  • Can exclude self-limited colitis
  • Usually without bowel prep

Colonoscopy

  • Helpful to determine extent
  • Contraindicated in severe exacerbation

Stool Studies

  • Stool culture
  • Microscopy
  • C. difficile toxin assay
  • Exclude infection

No single confirmatory test exists for UC

Diagnosis based on clinical, endoscopic, and histological findings

Clinical Pearl: In UC, non-bloody diarrhea is frequently the initial presentation; eventually progressing to bloody diarrhea
