Treatment: 5-ASA Derivatives

Mainstay for mild to moderate disease: 5-ASA (Mesalamine) derivatives

Topical (Suppository/Enema)

  • Effective for distal disease (rectum to splenic flexure)
  • Preferred over corticosteroids when inflammation is mild

Oral

  • Effective for mild to moderate colitis
  • Sulfasalazine 3-4 g/d
  • Mesalamine 4 g/d
  • Not effective in severe colitis

BENEFITS OF 5-ASA

  • Reduces yearly relapse rate from 60% to 15%
  • Commonly used for maintaining remission
  • May decrease rate of colorectal cancer
