Definition & Epidemiology

DEFINITION

Inflammatory disease affecting colonic mucosa anywhere from rectum (always involved) to cecum

EPIDEMIOLOGY

  • Incidence: 3-15 to 10-20/100,000 worldwide
  • 120,000 Canadians living with UC
  • 2/3 onset by age 30 (second peak after 50)
  • M = F
  • 15% have 1st degree relative with disease
  • Reduced risk in smokers

Inflammation limited to rectum or left colon is more common than pancolitis
