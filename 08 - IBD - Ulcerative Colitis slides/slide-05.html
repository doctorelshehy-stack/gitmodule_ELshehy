Clinical Features

Hallmark: Rectal bleeding is the cardinal feature

GI SYMPTOMS

  • Diarrhea (if more than rectum involved)
  • Abdominal cramps/pain (especially with defecation)
  • Tenesmus, urgency, incontinence
  • Bloody diarrhea (hallmark)

SYSTEMIC FEATURES

  • Fever
  • Anorexia
  • Weight loss
  • Fatigue (in severe cases)
  • EIMs (extraintestinal manifestations)

Pattern: Characteristic exacerbations and remissions; 5% of cases are fulminant
