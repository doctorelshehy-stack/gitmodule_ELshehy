Complete Summary

HE & HPS

Type A/B/C HE. Ammonia key toxin. Lactulose/Rifaximin. HPS: IPVDs, contrast echo, LTX if PaO₂>55-60.

Enteric Fever

3 stages. Rose spots. Widal test. 3rd/4th gen Ceph, FQ, AZM. Chronic salmonellosis + Praziquantel.

Brucellosis

Zoonotic. Undulant fever. Doxy+Strep/Rif ×6wk. Triple therapy for complicated. Valve surgery.

Schistosomiasis

3 species. Cercaria infective. Katayama syndrome. Urinary/intestinal/hepatosplenic. Praziquantel 40mg/kg single dose.

Malaria

P. falciparum most severe. ACT for uncomplicated. ABC prevention. Severe: IV Artesunate.

Toxoplasmosis & FUO

T. gondii. Cats. Serology IgM/IgG/avidity. Sulfadiazine+Pyrimethamine. FUO: ≥3wk, 4 categories, 3-level investigation.

Diarrheal Diseases

Acute vs chronic. Viral, bacterial, parasitic. EHEC: no Abx (HUS risk). C. diff: Metronidazole/Vancomycin. SIBO, Tropical Sprue, Intestinal TB, Whipple's.
