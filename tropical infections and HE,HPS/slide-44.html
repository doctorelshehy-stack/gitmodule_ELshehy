Section 7 • Diarrheal Diseases

Treatment of Acute Diarrhea

1. Fluid & Electrolyte Replacement

Assess dehydration degree. Replace deficits. Dehydration is the cause of death in acute diarrhea.

2. Diet

  • Calories needed for enterocyte renewal
  • Encourage: fruit drinks, tea, bananas, apple, rice, potatoes, noodles, toast, soups
  • Green bananas, pectins ↓ diarrhea amount/duration
  • Avoid: Dairy, caffeinated beverages, alcohol

3-5. Adjunctive

  • Anxiolytics (diazepam 2mg), antiemetics
  • Zinc supplementation for malnourished children (prevents recurrence, improves enterocyte renewal)

6. Antibiotics — Indications

  • Immunocompromised or elderly
  • Mechanical heart valves
  • Recent vascular grafts
  • Empirical: Ciprofloxacin 500mg bid ×3-5d
  • Suspected giardiasis: Metronidazole 250mg qid ×7d

Complications

  • Dehydration, Hypovolemic shock, Renal failure
  • Thromboembolism, Toxic megacolon
  • Convulsions, Overdehydration
  • Hypoglycemia, Malnutrition

Systemic Manifestations of Infectious Diarrhea

Reiter's syndrome: arthritis + urethritis + conjunctivitis (Salmonella, Campylobacter, Shigella, Yersinia).
HUS: EHEC O157:H7 and Shigella.
