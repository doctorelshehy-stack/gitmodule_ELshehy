Section 1 • Hepatic Encephalopathy

Type C HE — Further Classification

(a) Episodic HE

Short period episodes, fluctuating severity.

Precipitated: GI hemorrhage, uremia
Spontaneous: No recognized factors
Recurrent: 2 episodes within 1 year

(b) Persistent HE

Persistent cognitive deficits impacting social/occupational function.

Mild: Grade 1
Severe: Grades 2-4

(c) Minimal HE (MHE)

No recognizable clinical symptoms but mild cognitive/psychomotor deficits on specialized testing.

Key: Type A = Acute liver failure. Type B = Porto-systemic bypass without intrinsic liver disease. Type C = Cirrhosis + portal hypertension (most common context).
