Section 3 • Brucellosis

Treatment

A. Uncomplicated (6 weeks)

Regimen 1: Doxycyclin 200 mg/day PO + Streptomycin 1gm IM/day (21 days)

Regimen 2: Doxycyclin 200 mg/day PO + Rifampicin 600-900 mg/day (6 weeks)

Children ≤8yr: Co-trimoxazole (TMP 10mg/kg + SMZ 50mg/kg) + Rifampicin 10 mg/kg

B. Complicated (Endocarditis, Meningitis, Osteomyelitis)

  • Triple therapy: Rifampin + Doxycycline + Streptomycin
  • Duration: 4-6 months

C. Pregnancy

Rifampicin ± TMP-SMZ for 8-12 weeks. Avoid TMP-SMZ in first trimester and end of pregnancy (kernicterus risk).

Role of Surgery

  • Valve replacement for Brucella endocarditis (large, destructive aortic lesions)
  • Drainage of pyogenic joint effusions or para spinal abscesses
