Section 5 • Malaria

Prevention & Control

ABC of Malaria Prevention

A

Awareness

of risk

B

Bite

Mosquito repellents, permethrin-impregnated clothing, bed nets

C

Chemoprophylaxis

Chloroquine 500mg weekly (no resistance) or Mefloquine 250mg weekly (resistant areas)

Vaccine

Development of a fully protective malaria vaccine is still some way off (natural immunity is incomplete and not long-lived). Some evidence that vaccination can reduce incidence of severe malaria. Trial vaccines being evaluated in Africa.
