Section 1 • Hepatic Encephalopathy

Diagnosis of HE

Clinical diagnosis based on symptoms reported by patients and caregivers.

History / Symptoms

  • Confusion, lethargy, memory loss
  • Disorientation, slowness to respond
  • Personality change, increased aggression
  • Reversal of day/night sleep pattern
  • Poor compliance with lactulose or overconsumption of proteins (recurrent admissions)
  • Clonus in comatose patients (non-specific)

Physical Examination

  • Stigmata of CLD: jaundice, muscle wasting, gynecomastia, ascites, spider angioma, leg edema, splenomegaly
  • Fetor hepaticus: musty breath smell from gut-derived methanethiol
  • Asterixis: hyperactive deep tendon reflexes → stage 2 HE
