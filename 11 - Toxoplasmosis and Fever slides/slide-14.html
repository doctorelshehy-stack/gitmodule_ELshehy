FUO: Definition & Classification

Definition

It is a term best reserved for patient with a fever documented by a health care provider & fever has lasted for 3 or more weeks, with temperature > 38 degree C on most day and for which cause could not be identified after 3 weeks of evaluation as an outpatient or after 1 week of evaluation in hospital.

Classification (4 categories)

  1. Classic FUO
  2. Health care associated FUO
  3. Immune deficient FUO
  4. HIV-related FUO

Key criteria: Fever >38°C for ≥3 weeks | Cause unidentified after 3 weeks outpatient OR 1 week inpatient evaluation
