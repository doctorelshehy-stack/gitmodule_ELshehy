FUO: Treatment & Review Questions

Treatment

  • Symptomatic
  • Specific according to the cause such as:
    • Antibiotics for bacterial infections
    • Antituberculous for TB
    • Surgical drainage for localized pus collection
    • Praziquantil and steroids for Katayama fever

Review Questions

  1. Mention definition of fever
  2. List four patterns of fever and example of each pattern
  3. Define FUO and list 4 causes
  4. Mention 4 non-infective causes of FUO
  5. List 5 investigations to diagnose a case of fever
  6. List 3 infective causes of fever with eosinophilia and drugs used for one cause only

Fever with eosinophilia causes: Katayama fever, Fashiolasis, Trichinosis, Filariasis, Hydatid disease

Drug for Katayama fever: Praziquantil + steroids
