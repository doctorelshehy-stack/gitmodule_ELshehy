Complications of Gastroenteritis (2)

5. Electrolyte Disturbance

  • Hypokalemia: ↓↓ Intake & Intestinal loss, rapid correction of acidosis. Manifestations: Abdominal distension & paralytic ileus, cardiac arrhythmias, hypotonia
  • Hypocalcemia: Rapid correction of acidosis by bicarbonate. Manifestations: Tetany (Carpo-pedal spasm) & Convulsions
  • Hypo or hypernatremia

6. Bleeding

Cause: Hypoprothrombinemia (↓↓ Vitamin K) – DIC

Manifestations: Bleeding (?ICH)

7. Convulsions

Causes: Febrile, ICH, Metabolic (↓↓ Ca, ↓↓ Na, ↑↑ Na, hypoglycemia), Toxic (Salmonella, Shigella), CNS infection, incorrect management of dehydration leading to brain edema

8. Persistent Diarrhea

Cause: Lactose intolerance, persistent infection, cow’s milk protein allergy, bacterial overgrowth

Manifestations: Diarrhea ≥ 14 days

9. Malnutrition

Cause: ↓↓ Intake (Marasmus), wrong feeding (Kwashiorkor)

Manifestations: Wasting (Marasmus), Edema (Kwashiorkor)

10. Others

Septic complications, rectal prolapse, encephalopathy
