Additional Therapies

Zinc Supplementation

Strong evidence: Zinc reduces duration and severity of diarrhea, could prevent 300,000 deaths. WHO/UNICEF recommend all children with acute diarrhea in at-risk areas receive oral zinc for 10-14 days during and after diarrhea. 10 mg/day for infants <6 mo, 20 mg/day for those >6 mo.

Antiemetic Agents

Helpful in management of emesis. Ondansetron (0.15 mg/kg/dose) is effective with fewer side effects than metoclopramide.
