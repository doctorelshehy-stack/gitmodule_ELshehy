Persistent & Chronic Diarrhea

Persistent Diarrhea

  • Lactose intolerance
  • Cow milk protein allergy
  • Bacterial overgrowth
  • Persistent infection (Giardiasis)

Chronic Diarrhea

  • Chronic infection: TB
  • Malabsorption: Cystic fibrosis, Celiac disease, Chronic Cholestasis

Biochemical Changes in Gastroenteritis (GE)

Diarrhea → ↓↓ H2O, ↓↓ HCO3, ↓↓ K → Dehydration & Acidosis

Vomiting → ↓↓ H2O, ↓↓ HCl → Dehydration & Alkalosis

Net result of GE: Usually, diarrhea is more severe than vomiting → Dehydration & Acidosis.
