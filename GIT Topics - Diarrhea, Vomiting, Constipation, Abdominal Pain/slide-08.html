Investigations in Acute Diarrhea

For the Cause

  • Stool microscopy: Pus cells, pathogenic organisms (giardia), reducing substance, stool pH (for lactose intolerance in persistent diarrhea)
  • Stool culture: Typing of interspecies, identification of pathogenic organisms

For Complications

  • Electrolytes (Na, Ca, K)
  • ABG (Arterial Blood Gas)
  • Renal function tests
  • Coagulation profile (for bleeding cases)
  • Lumbar puncture (if CNS infection suspected)
  • Abdominal imaging: US, erect X-ray (for suspected obstruction or intussusception)
