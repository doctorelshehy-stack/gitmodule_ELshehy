Nutrition & Medications in Cirrhosis

Nutrition

  • Monitor and supplement fat-soluble vitamins
  • Supplement zinc
  • Maintain adequate caloric intake
  • High-protein diet (unless encephalopathy)
  • Avoid hepatotoxins — alcohol cessation critical

Medications — Important Rules

  • Acetaminophen: Safe up to 2 g/day
  • AVOID ASA/NSAIDs (risk of GI bleeding, renal impairment)
  • Aminoglycosides contraindicated
  • Oral hypoglycemics: if compensated; insulin if decompensated

Fetor Hepaticus

Results from portosystemic shunting of blood, which allows mercaptans to pass directly to the lungs. A sweet, musty breath odor in advanced liver disease.
