PEDIATRIC GASTRO-ENTEROLOGY, HEPATOLOGY & INFECTIOUS DISEASES

Cold Chain & Catch-up Vaccination

Cold Chain

System of storage & transport at low temperature to preserve potency.

Requirements: refrigerator, cold box, vaccine carrier, ice packs.

Damaged Vaccine Recognition (DPT, Hep B):

Undamaged: after shaking looks smooth & cloudy, clears after 30 min with no sediment

Damaged: granular particles after shaking, clears with thick sediment after 30 min

Catch-up (Unvaccinated Child ≥1 yr)

1st visit:

TOPV + DPT + Hepatitis B + BCG

2nd visit (after 1–2 months):

2nd doses of TOPV, DPT, Hepatitis B

3rd visit (after 1–2 months):

3rd doses of TOPV, DPT, Hepatitis B + Measles
