SECTION 02 — HEPATORENAL SYNDROME

TIPS & MARS

5) TIPS

Transjugular Intrahepatic Portosystemic Shunt

  • It may improve renal perfusion and reduce the activity of RAAS
  • The 1 year survival was better in type 2 than type 1 (70% vs 20%)

6) MARS

Molecular Absorbent Recirculating System

  • This modified dialysis method uses an albumin-containing dialysate
  • This system has shown benefit for patients with type 1 HRS

TIPS Outcomes

  • Type 1: 1-year survival 20%
  • Type 2: 1-year survival 70%
  • Improves renal perfusion
  • Reduces RAAS activity

MARS Outcomes

  • Beneficial for type 1 HRS
  • Albumin-containing dialysate
  • Modified dialysis method
  • Bridge to transplantation
