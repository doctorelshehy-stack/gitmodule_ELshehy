SECTION 02 — HEPATORENAL SYNDROME

Classification

Type 1

  • Rapidly progressive (<2 weeks) reduction of renal functions with doubling of the initial SCr to greater than 2.5 mg/dl or a 50% reduction in the initial 24-h creatinine clearance to less than 20 ml/min
  • 80% mortality with only 10% surviving more than 3 months

Type 2

  • Patients satisfy the criteria of diagnosis but the renal failure does not progress rapidly
  • These patients usually have relatively preserved hepatic functions with refractory ascites
  • Survival is reduced compared with cirrhotics with ascites but normal renal function
  • Median survival for untreated type 2 approximately 6 months
