SECTION 01 — TB PERITONITIS

Investigations

1) Routine Investigations

ESR, CBC, LFTs, KFTs, Alb/creat ratio, HIV, Tuberculin test

2) Radiological

Abdominal US: Ascites, Plain chest x ray and Pelviabdomenal CT showing:

  • Peritoneal thickening
  • Free or loculated ascites
  • Mesenteric or omental thickening
  • Mesenteric or omental LNs

3) Ascitic Fluid Analysis

Chemistry

High protein >3 gm
SAAG <1.1
Adenosine deaminase >39 U/L

Cytology

Predominant lymphocytes

Bacteriology

Culture
Zeilnelssin stain

Molecular

PCR and GeneXpert MTB/RIF assay

4) Laparoscopy & Peritoneal Biopsy — The Gold Standard

Shows the granuloma of TB.
