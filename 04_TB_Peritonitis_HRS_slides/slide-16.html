SECTION 02 — HEPATORENAL SYNDROME

Type 2 Treatment & Prevention

► Treatment for Type 2 HRS

Management is focused mainly on the treatment of refractory ascites by LVP, TIPS or liver transplantation

Hemodialysis

Not effective in patients with HRS. Only used in specific clinical indications (hypervolemia, hyperkalemia, tubular acidosis) — bridging to liver transplantation.

► Prevention of HRS

Therapeutic Paracentesis

Administration of plasma volume expanders, specifically albumin after total paracentesis > 5 L

SBP (Spontaneous Bacterial Peritonitis)

Administration of albumin (1.5 gm/kg at diagnosis of infection & 1 gm/kg IV /48 hr later with cefotaxime)
