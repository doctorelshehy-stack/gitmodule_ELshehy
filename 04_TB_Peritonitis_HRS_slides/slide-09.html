SECTION 02 — HEPATORENAL SYNDROME

Definition

It is the development of severe renal failure in patients with severe liver disease in the absence of any identifiable renal pathology.

Epidemiology

  • About 20% of cirrhotic patients and normal renal function develop HRS after 1 year of follow up
  • 39% after 5 years

Prognosis

  • Without liver transplantation recovery of renal function was unusual (<5% of patients)
  • The prognosis was poor with a median survival after diagnosis of < 2 weeks
