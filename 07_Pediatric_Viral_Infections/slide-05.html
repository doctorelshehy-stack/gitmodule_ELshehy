Measles — Investigations & Complications

Investigations

Not necessary for diagnosis. Leukopenia with relative lymphocytosis. Rise of anti-measles Ab titer. Virus isolation from blood, urine, naso-pharyngeal secretions.

Respiratory

Pneumonia, otitis media, laryngitis & laryngo-tracho-bronchitis, sinusitis, tracheitis, bronchitis

Gastrointestinal

Thrush, ulcerative stomatitis, gastroenteritis, mesenteric adenitis, acute appendicitis

Neurological

Viral encephalitis, aseptic meningitis, post-infectious encephalitis, SSPE, MS, GBS, hemiplegia, retro-bulbar neuritis
