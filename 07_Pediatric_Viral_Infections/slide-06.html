Measles — Prevention & Treatment

Active Immunization

Living attenuated virus, 0.5 ml SC injection

Passive Immunization

Immune Serum Globulin (ISG) within 5 days after exposure

Prevention: ¼ ml/kg IM

Attenuation: 0.05 ml/kg IM (early incubation)

Treatment

• Isolation till 3 days after rash appearance

• Symptomatic: bed rest, adequate fluids, light diet

• Antipyretics, sedatives, cough mixtures

• Antibiotics if secondary infection

• Vitamin A — inverse correlation between retinol and severity; reduces morbidity & mortality in developing countries
