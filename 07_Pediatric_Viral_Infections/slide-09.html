PEDIATRIC GASTRO-ENTEROLOGY, HEPATOLOGY & INFECTIOUS DISEASES

Congenital Rubella Syndrome

Prenatally acquired, mostly 1st trimester. Virus isolable from secretions for up to 2 years. Dx: rubella-specific IgM in neonatal serum.

Eye Defects

Deafness

CNS

MR, microcephaly, convulsions, chronic persistent encephalitis

Treatment

No specific treatment. Medical support (blood transfusion, platelets). Newborn highly infectious for up to 2 years — avoid contact with pregnant females.
