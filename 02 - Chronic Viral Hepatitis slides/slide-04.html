Definition & Classification of Chronic Hepatitis

Definition of Chronic Hepatitis

Chronic hepatitis is defined as hepatic inflammation for at least 6 months.

New Classification (Current)

The new classification of chronic hepatitis is based on three key parameters:

History

Clinical history and etiology

Grade

Histological activity or grade of inflammation

Stage

Stage or degree of progression (fibrosis)
