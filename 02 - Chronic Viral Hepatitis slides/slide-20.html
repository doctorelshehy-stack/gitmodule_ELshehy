Summary & Key Takeaways

Key Takeaways — Chronic Viral Hepatitis

01 Definition

Hepatic inflammation ≥ 6 months. Old classification: CPH, CAH, CLH. New: based on history, grade, stage.

02 HBV

DNA virus (Hepadnaviridae). 3 antigens: HBsAg, HBcAg, HBeAg. 240M chronic carriers. 15-40% develop serious sequelae.

03 HBV Transmission

Parenteral, sexual, perinatal (vertical). Risk of chronicity: 80-90% infants, 30-50% children, <5% adults.

04 HBV Diagnosis

Serology (HBsAg, anti-HBs, anti-HBc IgM/IgG, HBeAg) → PCR → Liver biopsy (grade & stage).

05 HBV Prevention & Treatment

Vaccine + HBIG. Treatment: interferons, nucleos(t)ide analogues (lamivudine, adefovir, tenofovir, entecavir).

06 HCV

RNA virus (Flaviviridae). 6 genotypes. 180M carriers. 85% chronicity. No vaccine. DAAs curative (95%).
