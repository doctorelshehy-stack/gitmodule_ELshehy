HCV — Transmission & Natural History

Mode of Transmission

Intravenous Drug Use

Primary route of transmission in many regions.

Healthcare Exposure

Blood transfusion, transfusion of blood products.

Hemodialysis

Patients on long-term hemodialysis at risk.

Accidental Injuries

Needles/sharps injuries in healthcare settings.

Sexual/Household

Exposure to anti-HCV-positive contact (less efficient than HBV).

Natural History (Fate)
