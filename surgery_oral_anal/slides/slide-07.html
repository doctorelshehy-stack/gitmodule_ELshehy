Salivary Gland Neoplasms

Incidence by Site
• Parotid: 70% of neoplasms
• Submandibular: 22%
• Sublingual + Minor: 8%
Malignancy Risk:
• Parotid: 80% benign / 20% malignant
• Submandibular: 50/50
• Minor: 25% benign / 75% malignant
Classification
Benign:
• Pleomorphic adenoma (commonest)
• Monomorphic adenoma
• Warthin's tumor
• Adenolymphoma, Oncocytoma
Malignant:
• Mucoepidermoid carcinoma (commonest)
• Adenoid cystic carcinoma (cylindroma)
• Acinic cell carcinoma
• Adenocarcinoma
• Carcinoma ex pleomorphic adenoma
• Lymphoma
DD of Parotid Mass
Parietal: lipoma, neurofibroma
Musculoskeletal:
• Masseter: fibrosarcoma, hypertrophy
• Bone: Burkitt's lymphoma
Pre-auricular LN:
• Lymphadenitis (acute/chronic)
• Lymphoma, metastatic carcinoma
Parotid:
• Inflammation: viral, bacterial, TB
• Autoimmune: Sjogren
• Tumors (see classification)
