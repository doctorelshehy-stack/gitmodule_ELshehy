Treatment — Severe & Fulminant Hepatitis

Fulminant Hepatitis Management

If doctors suspect that fulminant hepatitis is developing, the person is hospitalized so that:

  • Mental status can be monitored
  • Liver tests can be done
  • Doctors can determine whether liver transplantation is needed

HBV Fulminant Hepatitis

  • Treated in intensive care unit (ICU)
  • Antiviral drugs may help
  • Monitor liver function and coagulation

Liver Transplantation

  • The most effective treatment
  • Best hope of survival, especially for adults
  • Indicated when medical management fails

Important: No alcohol until fully recovered. Infected liver may not metabolize drugs normally — dosage adjustment is often required.
