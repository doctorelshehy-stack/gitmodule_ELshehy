Prevention of Acute Viral Hepatitis — Vaccines

Because treatments for viral hepatitis are only partially effective, preventing viral hepatitis is very important.

✓

Vaccines Available

  • Hepatitis A vaccine
  • Hepatitis B vaccine

✗

No Vaccines Available

  • Hepatitis C
  • Hepatitis D

Hepatitis E vaccine is currently available only in China.

Vaccination against HBV also reduces the risk of infection with HDV.

Vaccination Schedule & Recommendations:

  • Hepatitis vaccines are given by injection into muscle.
  • Routine vaccination (HAV and HBV) recommended for all children and for adults at high risk.
  • Protection requires several weeks for the vaccine to reach its full effect as the immune system gradually creates antibodies.
  • Post-exposure for HAV (unvaccinated): single dose of HAV vaccine or injection of standard immune globulin (depending on age and health).
