Epidemiology of Viral Hepatitis

High-Income Regions

Generally, the rates of viral hepatitis are low in high-income regions.

Resource-Poor Areas

Rates are high in resource-poor areas.

The past five years have seen a small increase in cases, mostly from:

  • Isolated food-related outbreaks
  • Persons who use drugs
  • The homeless population

Key fact: In high endemic areas, HAV has affected more than 90% of children by age 10.
