Treatment of Acute Viral Hepatitis — Supportive Care

For most people with acute viral hepatitis, special treatment is not necessary. However, people with severe acute hepatitis may require hospitalization so that symptoms can be treated.

✅ Recommended

  • Appetite usually returns after the first several days
  • No need to stay in bed
  • Return to work after jaundice clears (even if LFTs not quite normal)
  • Cholestyramine (by mouth) for itching

⚠️ Important Precautions

  • No alcohol until fully recovered
  • Drug dosage adjustment: infected liver may not metabolize drugs normally
  • Doctor may need to stop or reduce drugs that accumulate to harmful levels (e.g., warfarin, theophylline)
  • Tell doctor ALL drugs (prescription, non-prescription, medicinal herbs)

Key principle: Severe restrictions of diet or activity are unnecessary, and vitamin supplements are not required.
