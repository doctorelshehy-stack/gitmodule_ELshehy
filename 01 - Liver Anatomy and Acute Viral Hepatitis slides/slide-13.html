Lab Diagnosis: Hepatitis C & E

Laboratory Diagnosis: HCV & HEV

Hepatitis C (HCV)

Anti-HCV

  • Cannot distinguish acute from chronic infection

HCV Core Antigen Assay

  • May be used as an alternative marker

PCR (Viral RNA)

  • Used to detect viral RNA
  • HCV RNA is the most accurate marker for HCV infection

Hepatitis E (HEV)

Anti-HEV Antibodies

  • IgM — indicates acute infection
  • IgG — indicates past/chronic infection

PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction)

  • Polymerase chain detection assay
  • Detects HEV RNA

HCV: PCR is the gold standard

HEV: Serology + PCR confirmation
