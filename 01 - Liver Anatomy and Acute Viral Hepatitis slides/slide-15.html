Prevention — Immune Globulin & Other Measures

Immune Globulin

HBV Exposure

If unvaccinated people are exposed to HBV, they are given hepatitis B immune globulin AND are vaccinated. Given by injection.

Babies Born to HBV+ Mothers

Given hepatitis B immune globulin (injected into muscle) AND hepatitis B vaccine.

Other Preventive Measures:

🧼 Wash hands before handling food
💉 Do not share needles to inject drugs
🪥 Do not share toothbrushes, razors, or blood items
🛡️ Practice safe sex (condom, limit partners)
🩸 Donated blood is screened; rarely contaminated
🏥 Transfusions only when essential
📅 Pre-surgery: autologous blood donation

Summary: Prevention includes vaccines (HAV, HBV), immune globulin (post-exposure), and behavioral measures (hygiene, safe sex, needle safety, blood screening).
