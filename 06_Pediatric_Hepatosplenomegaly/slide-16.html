PEDIATRIC GASTRO-ENTEROLOGY, HEPATOLOGY & INFECTIOUS DISEASES

Key Points & Summary

Hepatomegaly Mechanisms

6 mechanisms: congestion, inflammation, infiltration, storage/fat, proliferation, SOL

Palpable ≠ Enlarged

Always assess vertical span. Ptosis: pneumothorax, empyema, emphysema

Splenomegaly

>1 cm below costal margin in child >6mo. Evaluate by US + age charts

Massive vs Mild

Massive: EHPVO, CML, Gaucher | Mild: infections, thalassemia, ITP

HSM Causes

Infections, metabolic, vascular, hematological, storage, malignancy

DDx by Associated Feature

Fever, GI bleed, jaundice, anemia, FTT, lymphadenopathy, neurological, renal
