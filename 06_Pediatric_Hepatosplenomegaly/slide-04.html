PEDIATRIC GASTRO-ENTEROLOGY, HEPATOLOGY & INFECTIOUS DISEASES

Mechanisms of Hepatomegaly

1. Congestion

CCF, Hepatic Venous Outflow Obstruction (HVOO)

2. Inflammation

Various forms of hepatitis (viral, autoimmune, drug-induced)

3. Infiltration

Malignancy (leukemia, lymphoma, metastases)

4. Storage / Fat Accumulation

Glycogen storage disease, fatty liver

5. Proliferation of:

• Liver cells → hepatoma
• Hematopoietic elements → hemolytic anemia
• Kupffer cells → septicemia, acute hepatitis
• ITO cells → vitamin A toxicity
• Multiple cell types → congenital hepatic fibrosis, biliary atresia

6. Space Occupying Lesion (SOL)

Choledochal cyst, infected cyst, hydatid cyst, liver abscess
