PEDIATRIC GASTRO-ENTEROLOGY, HEPATOLOGY & INFECTIOUS DISEASES

Moderate / Mild Splenomegaly — Causes

Category Causes
Infections Malaria, typhoid, HIV, viral illness
Portal HTN Portal hypertension (any cause)
Hematological Thalassemia trait, iron deficiency anemia, hereditary spherocytosis, ITP, juvenile rheumatoid arthritis
Storage Disorder Various inborn errors of metabolism
