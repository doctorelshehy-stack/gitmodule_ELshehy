PEDIATRIC GASTRO-ENTEROLOGY, HEPATOLOGY & INFECTIOUS DISEASES

Typhoid — Treatment

Specific Treatment (2 weeks)

Fever usually returns to normal within 5–7 days.

First Line

Ceftriaxone: 50–80 mg/kg/day single dose IV

Alternatives (resistant/toxic)

Ampicillin 100–200 mg/kg/d
Amoxicillin 100 mg/kg/d
Co-trimoxazole (TMP-SMX)

General Measures

• Bed rest

• Light diet with excess fluids during fever

• Antipyretics: Paracetamol 10–15 mg/kg/dose, up to 5 times/day
