PEDIATRIC GASTRO-ENTEROLOGY, HEPATOLOGY & INFECTIOUS DISEASES

Bacterial Meningitis — Treatment

⚠ Medical Emergency — IV infusion after LP

Infants <6 months

Ampicillin + Gentamicin IV

Older infants & children

Ampicillin + Chloramphenicol IV

Dosing

• Ampicillin: initial 50 mg/kg, then 50–75 mg/kg every 6h

• Chloramphenicol: initial 50 mg/kg, then 25 mg/kg every 6h

• Gentamicin: newborns 2.5 mg/kg every 12h; older: 2.5 mg/kg every 8h

Supportive Measures

• Decrease raised intracranial pressure (mannitol, dexamethasone)

• Treat hypovolemic shock

• Corticosteroids for severe cases

• Treat complications

Treatment duration: Adjust based on CSF response. Change antibiotics per sensitivity.
