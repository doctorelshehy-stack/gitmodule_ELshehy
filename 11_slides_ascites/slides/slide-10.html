Management Overview

Transudative

  • Restricting sodium and water intake
  • Promoting urine output by diuretics
  • Removing ascites directly by paracentesis

Exudative (Malignancy)

  • Paracentesis without fluid replacement
  • Ovarian cancer: US-guided paracentesis

General Principles

  • Weigh the patient regularly
  • Avoid removing more than 1 L of fluid daily (1 kg)
  • Weight loss target: 0.5 kg/day without edema, 1 kg/day with peripheral edema
