Investigations — US & Paracentesis

Ultrasound

  • Used in obese patients
  • Detects small volume ascites
  • Guides paracentesis procedure

Pleural Effusion

  • Mostly right side
  • "Hepatic hydrothorax"
  • Identified by chest X-ray

Diagnostic Paracentesis

Confirms ascites and obtains fluid for analysis.

Key measurements:

  • Protein concentration
  • Serum-Ascites Albumin Gradient (SAAG)

Cirrhotic patients: Transudate with total protein mostly below 25 g/L and relatively few cells.
