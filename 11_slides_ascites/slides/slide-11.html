Sodium & Water Restriction

Diet & Fluid Restriction

  • Sodium intake: 100 mmol/day (no added salt diet)
  • Water restriction (1–1.5 L/day) only if plasma Na <125 mmol/L

High Sodium Content (Avoid)

Medications:

  • Antacids
  • Alginates
  • Antibiotics
  • Phenytoin
  • Sodium valproate
  • Effervescent preparations

Sodium Retention:

  • Carbenoxolone
  • Corticosteroids
  • Metoclopramide
  • NSAIDs
  • Estrogens

Key Points

Restriction of sodium intake to 100 mmol/day (no added salt diet) is usually adequate.

Water restriction (1–1.5 L/day) is necessary only if plasma sodium falls below 125 mmol/L.
