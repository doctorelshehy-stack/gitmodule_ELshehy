SECTION 05 — INVESTIGATIONS

Investigations — Imaging

3) Imaging Modalities

Abdominal Ultrasound

▸Detect liver cirrhosis: echopattern, size (shrunken, average, or enlarged)
▸Portal hypertension: dilated portal vein and splenomegaly
▸Presence of ascites
▸Hepatic focal lesions

Triphasic CT / MRI

▸For abdomen and pelvis
▸In suspected cases with HCC (Hepatocellular Carcinoma)
▸Provides detailed characterization of liver lesions
▸Assesses vascular involvement and metastasis
