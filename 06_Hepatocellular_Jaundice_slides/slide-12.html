SECTION 04 — DIAGNOSIS

Clinical Presentations

❖ General Manifestations

▸Symptoms and signs of chronic liver disease
▸Fatigue, malaise, anorexia
▸Weight loss
▸Right upper quadrant discomfort

❖ Specific Manifestations of Hepatocellular Jaundice

▸Orange-yellow sclera
▸Pale stool (acholic stool)
▸Dark urine (bilirubinuria)

❖ Manifestations of Complications

▸HCC — Hepatocellular Carcinoma
▸HRS — Hepatorenal Syndrome
▸SBP — Spontaneous Bacterial Peritonitis
▸GI bleeding as hematemesis due to esophageal varices
