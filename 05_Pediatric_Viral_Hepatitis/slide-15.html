PEDIATRIC GASTRO-ENTEROLOGY, HEPATOLOGY & INFECTIOUS DISEASES

HBV Vaccine — Detailed Information

Vaccine Type

Recombinant (engineered) vaccine

HBsAg produced in yeast cells

Route & Dose

Intramuscular (IM), 0.5 ml per dose

Schedule

3 doses: 2, 4, 6 months

Also: 0, 1, 6 months (alternate schedule)

Neonatal Dose

HBIG + HBV vaccine within 12 hrs of birth

For infants born to HBsAg+ mothers

Key Points

• Most effective way to prevent chronic HBV, cirrhosis, and HCC

• Also protects against HDV (no HBV = no HDV replication)

• Seroprotection: anti-HBs >10 mIU/mL after completion

• Booster doses may be needed for non-responders

• Universal neonatal vaccination in many countries
