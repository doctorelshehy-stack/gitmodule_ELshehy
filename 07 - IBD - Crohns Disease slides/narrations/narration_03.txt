Let us start with the big picture of inflammatory bowel disease! Inflammatory bowel disease or IBD has a complex cause. It is not just one thing it is a combination of genetics the immune system and the bacteria that normally live in your gut. In a healthy person the immune system knows when to calm down after fighting an infection. In IBD that calming signal does not work properly. Genetics play a big role. If you have a family member with Crohn disease your risk is higher. There is one gene in particular called CARD15 that is very important. People with one copy have three times the risk of developing Crohn disease. People with two copies have forty times the risk! This gene helps the immune system respond to bacteria. When it is not working right the gut cannot control inflammation properly.
