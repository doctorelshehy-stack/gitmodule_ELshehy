Hey there! Welcome to our adventure into Crohn disease! Crohn disease is a type of inflammatory bowel disease that can affect the entire digestive tract from your mouth all the way to the other end. It is a chronic condition which means it lasts a long time and it can cause inflammation ulcers and even holes called fistulas in the bowel. Today we will learn about what causes it who gets it the symptoms to watch for and the many treatments available. There is a lot to discover let us dive in!
