MELD-Na Score & Prognosis

MELD-Na Score

Used to:

  • Stratify liver transplantation list
  • Predict 3-month survival in cirrhosis
  • Predict outcomes in some acute liver disease

Based on:

Creatinine INR Bilirubin Na

Management Principles

  1. Treatment of causes — address underlying etiology
  2. Maintenance of nutrition — prevent malnutrition
  3. Treatment of complications — ascites, varices, encephalopathy
  4. Orthotopic liver transplantation (OLT) — for chronic liver failure
