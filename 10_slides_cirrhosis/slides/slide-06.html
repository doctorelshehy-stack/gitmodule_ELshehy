Investigations — LFTs & Labs

LFTs

  • Elevated bilirubin
  • Prolonged PT/INR
  • Hypoalbuminemia (<30 g/L)
  • ± Elevated aminotransferases (AST > ALT if late)

Hematologic

  • Anemia (marrow suppression, hypersplenism, iron/folate deficiency)
  • Neutropenia (hypersplenism)
  • Thrombocytopenia (hypersplenism)

Chemical

  • Hyponatremia (↑ ADH due to ↓ EAV)
  • ↑ Fe/TIBC
  • ↑ Ferritin (released from hepatocytes)

Poor Prognosis Markers

  • Deteriorating liver function
  • Jaundice
  • Ascites or encephalopathy
  • Marked hyponatremia (<120 mmol/L) not due to diuretics
  • Prolonged PTT

Lab Indices Predictive of Cirrhosis

  • AST/plt ratio > 2
  • Indirect markers of fibrosis:
    • FibroTest/FibroSURE
    • FIB-4 index
    • NAFLD fibrosis score
