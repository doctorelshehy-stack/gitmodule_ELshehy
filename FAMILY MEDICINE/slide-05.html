Classification of Causes by Site of Pain

RUQ (Right Upper Quadrant)

Acute cholecystitis, Duodenal ulcer, Hepatitis, Congestive hepatomegaly, Pyelonephritis, (R) Pneumonia

Epigastrium

Perforated oesophagus, Gastric ulcer, Aortic aneurysm, Perforated colon, Pyelonephritis, (L) Pneumonia

LUQ (Left Upper Quadrant)

Ruptured spleen, Intestinal obstruction, Acute pancreatitis

Midabdominal

Early appendicitis, Mesenteric thrombosis, Aortic aneurysm

RLQ (Right Lower Quadrant)

Appendicitis, Diverticulitis, Salpingitis, Tubo-ovarian abscess, Ruptured ectopic pregnancy, Renal/ureteric stone

LLQ (Left Lower Quadrant)

Sigmoid diverticulitis, Incarcerated hernia, Perforated caecum, Psoas abscess, Renal/ureteral stone, Crohn's disease
