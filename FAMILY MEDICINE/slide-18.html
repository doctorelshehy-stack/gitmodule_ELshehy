Constipation - Definition & Causes

📝 Definition
  • Less than 3 stools per week
  • Other criteria: lumpy/hard stools, straining, sensation of incomplete evacuation
  • Need to use manual maneuvers such as digital extraction
📈 Incidence
  • Common in extreme ages of life
  • Occurs in 15% of adults
  • More common in women
  • Poor fluid intake and lack of fiber are common causes
⚠ Causes
  1. Insufficient dietary fiber
  2. Inactivity
  3. Medications: Opiates, CCB, Anticholinergics, Tricyclic antidepressants, Diuretics, Antacids, Clonidine, Levodopa, Laxative abuse
  4. Metabolic: Hypokalemia, Hypercalcemia, Hypothyroidism, Scleroderma, Amyloidosis, Pregnancy
  5. Neurologic: Parkinson disease, Hirschsprung disease
  6. Structural: Colonic mass, stricture, Fissure, Hemorrhoids, Rectocele, Rectal prolapse, Anorectal stricture
  7. Psychological: Depression
  8. DM, IBS, Prior pelvic surgery
