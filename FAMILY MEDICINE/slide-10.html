Investigation

🔬 Laboratory Tests
  • Blood tests: fasting blood glucose, amylase, glucose, clotting, occasionally Ca2+, (ABG)
  • Blood cultures
  • Pregnancy test in women of childbearing age
  • Urine analysis
🗡 Imaging
  • X-ray (supine)
  • Chest X-ray (erect looking for gas under diaphragm)
  • CT scan
  • Ultrasound
📜 Additional Tests
  • ECG and cardiac enzymes
  • Peritoneal lavage if history of abdominal trauma
