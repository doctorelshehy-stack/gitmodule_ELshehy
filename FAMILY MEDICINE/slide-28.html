Weight Loss - Medication Adverse Effects

⚠ Adverse Effects Leading to Weight Loss

Altered taste or smell

Allopurinol, ACE inhibitors, antibiotics, anticholinergics, antihistamines, CCB, levodopa, propranolol, selegiline, spironolactone

Anorexia

Amantadine, antibiotics, anticonvulsants, antipsychotics, benzodiazepines, digoxin, levodopa, metformin, neuroleptics, opiates, SSRIs, theophylline

Dry mouth

Anticholinergics, antihistamines, clonidine, loop diuretics

💉 Additional AE Categories

Dysphagia

Bisphosphonates, doxycycline, gold, iron, NSAIDs, potassium

Nausea and vomiting

Amantadine, antibiotics, bisphosphonates, digoxin, dopamine agonists, metformin, SSRIs, statins, tricyclic antidepressants

📝 Medications Causing Weight Loss
  • Antiepileptic drugs
  • Selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors
  • Bupropion
  • Metformin and GLP-1 agonists
  • Diuretics and laxatives
  • Antibiotics (through diarrhea or direct effects)
