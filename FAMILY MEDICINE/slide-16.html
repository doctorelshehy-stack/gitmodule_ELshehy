CRC Management & Prevention

💉 Management
  1. Wide surgical resection of lesion and regional lymphatic drainage
  2. For cancer rectum: abdominoperineal resection or low anterior resection
  3. Radiochemotherapy can improve survival
  4. Colonic stents for acute large bowel obstruction
  5. CT for diagnosis of mechanical obstruction, metastatic disease or perforation
📅 Screening Intervals
  • HSgFOBT or FIT: every year
  • CT colonography: every 5 years
  • Flexible sigmoidoscopy: every 5 years
  • Flexible sigmoidoscopy + FIT: every 10 years + FIT every year
  • Colonoscopy: every 10 years
💚 Prevention
  1. Most effective: get screened routinely beginning at age 45
  2. Diet: low in animal fats, high in fruits, vegetables, and whole grains
  3. Low-dose aspirin
  4. Physical activity
  5. Prevent alcohol consumption
  6. Avoiding tobacco
📄 AGEA Guidelines
  • One family history: colonoscopy start at age 40, every 5-10 years
  • >2 first-degree relatives or >1 diagnosed before age 60: colonoscopy at 40 or 10 years before youngest diagnosis
