Weight Loss - Definition & Pathophysiology

📝 Definition
  • Unintentional weight loss: >5% loss in body weight within 6 to 12 months
  • Weight loss not intended by patient, not consequence of expected treatment
  • Occurs in 15% to 20% of older adults
  • May indicate presence of significant underlying illness
  • Associated with increased morbidity and mortality
⚠ Consequences
  1. Decreased daily activities
  2. Cachexia (loss of muscle mass with or without loss of fat)
  3. Increased morbidity and mortality
  4. Increased risk of infections, pressure ulcers, osteoporosis
  5. Failure to respond to medical treatments
📈 Pathophysiology
  • Diminished intake: decreased interest in food, inability to obtain food, pain with food ingestion
  • Malabsorption: hepatic and intestinal disorder
  • Excessive loss of nutrients: recurrent vomiting or diarrhea
  • Increased caloric expenditure: hyperthyroidism, excessive exercise, malignancy
📌 Etiology Categories
  • Organic causes
  • Psychosocial causes
  • Idiopathic causes
