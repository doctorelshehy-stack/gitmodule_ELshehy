Assessment & History Taking

🔬 Initial Impression
  1. Does patient look ill, septic or shocked?
  2. Lying still (peritonitis) or writhing (colic)?
  3. Assess and manage ABC as priority
  4. Community setting: rapid transfer to hospital
📝 History Components
  • Demographic details, occupation, recent travel
  • History of recent abdominal trauma
  • Pain analysis: (SOCRATES)
📈 Associated Symptoms
  • Vomiting and nature of vomitus
  • Hematemesis or melena
  • New lumps in abdominal region
  • Diarrhea, constipation
  • Fainting, dizziness or palpitations
  • Fever/rigors, Rash or itching
  • Urinary symptoms, Recent weight loss
  • Past medical and surgical history
