Section 1 • Hepatopulmonary Syndrome

Treatment of HPS & HPS Diagnosis Flowchart

Lines of Treatment

Conservative

Oxygen can be used in all patients

Medical

Not effective

Surgical

Liver transplant in patients with PaO2 > 55-60 mmHg and shunt fraction < 20%

HPS Diagnosis Flowchart (from image)

Chronic liver disease → Pulse oximetry
  • SaO2 ≥ 96% → No severe gas exchange abnormality
  • SaO2 < 96% → Contrast echocardiography
     IPVD positive → Arterial blood gas analysis
     IPVD negative → No HPS
       • AaPO2 < 15 mmHg → No HPS, solely IPVD
       • AaPO2 ≥ 15, PaO2 ≥ 80 → Mild HPS
       • AaPO2 ≥ 15, PaO2 < 80 → PFT + chest imaging
          • PaO2 < 60 → Severe HPS, LTOT, evaluate for LTX
          • PaO2 ≥ 60 → Moderate HPS, follow up
