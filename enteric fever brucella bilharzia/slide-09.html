Section 1 • Hepatic Encephalopathy

Treatment Summary — Three-Pronged Approach

General Treatment

  • Adequate caloric intake: 35-45 kcal/kg/day
  • Protein intake: 1-1.5 g protein/kg bodyweight
  • Vegetable protein may be superior to animal protein
  • High fiber diet encouraged
  • Lactulose 15-30 cm³ q4-6h to ensure 2-3 bowel movements/day
  • Rifaximin 550 mg PO bid

Treatment of Cause

  • Infection: Antibiotics
  • GI Bleeding due to OVs: Band ligation or injection sclerotherapy

Treatment of Complication

  • Surgical: Embolization of large portosystemic collaterals in patient with spontaneous chronic incapacitating encephalopathy
  • Liver transplantation
