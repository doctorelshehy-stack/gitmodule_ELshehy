Causes of Lower GI Bleeding

1. Colonic Diverticula

Most common cause of LGIB, painless brisk bleeding

2. Colonic Ischemia

Abdominal pain with varying degrees of bleeding

3. Angiomas

Angiodysplasia — painless, intermittent bleeding

4. Hemorrhoids

Bright red blood per rectum, common in adults

5. Colorectal Cancer

Rarely causes significant LGIB, chronic blood loss

6. NSAIDs Colo-pathy

Medication-induced mucosal damage

7. Vascular Lesions

Includes Dieulafoy lesion, angiectasia

8. Post-Polypectomy

Bleeding after polyp removal procedure

Colonic diverticula and angiodysplasia are the most common causes of LGIB. NSAIDs colo-pathy is an increasingly recognized iatrogenic cause.
