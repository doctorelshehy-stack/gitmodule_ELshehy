Surgery Indications

Indications for surgical intervention in LGIB:

Active Persistent Bleeding

Hemodynamic instability refractory to aggressive resuscitation

Persistent Recurrent Bleeding

Continued or returning bleeding despite conservative measures

Massive Transfusion (24h)

Transfusion of >4 units pRBCs in 24 hours with active/recurrent bleeding

Cumulative Transfusion

Transfusion of >6 units pRBCs during the same hospitalization

Surgery is considered when conservative and interventional radiology measures fail to achieve hemostasis.
