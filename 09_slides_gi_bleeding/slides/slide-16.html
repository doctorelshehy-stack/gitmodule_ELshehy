Mid-GI Diagnostic Tools

The diagnosis and treatment of mid GI bleeding has been completely modified by important tools after normal upper and lower endoscopy:

Push Endoscopy

Extended upper endoscopy into small bowel

Double Balloon Endoscopy

Deep small bowel visualization

Capsule Endoscopy

Non-invasive small bowel imaging

Imaging Studies

CT, MRI, barium studies

Angiography

Diagnostic & therapeutic

Bleeding Scan

Labeled RBCs scan

Rarely: Surgery with intraoperative endoscopy can be performed in truly hemodynamically unstable patients owing to the speed and volume of bleeding.
