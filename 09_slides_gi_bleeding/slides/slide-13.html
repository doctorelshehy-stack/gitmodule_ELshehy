Hemostasis by Cause

Diverticular Bleeding

  • Colonoscopy with bipolar probe coagulation
  • Epinephrine injection
  • Metallic clips
  • If recurrent: resection of affected bowel segment

Angiodysplasia

  • Thermal therapy:
    • Electrocoagulation
    • Argon plasma coagulation (APC)

Ischemic Colitis

  • Conservative management
  • NPO (nothing by mouth)
  • IV hydration

Unknown Bleeding Source

  • Vasoconstrictive agents: Vasopressin (Pitressin)
  • If vasopressin unsuccessful/contraindicated:
  • → Interventional radiology with super selective embolization
