AIH — Prognosis & Overlap Syndromes

Prognosis

  • 60–85% achieve remission (normalization of enzymes)
  • Normalization of gamma globulins
  • Improved histology — non/minimal interface hepatitis
  • Remission at 18–24 months
  • 80% remission with steroids (1.5–2 years)
  • Cirrhotic patients: may develop decompensation
  • 10% fail steroids → ESLD

Overlap Syndromes (18% of AIH)

AIH–PBC Overlap

  • Type 1: AMA+, AIH histology, steroid responsive
  • Type 2: AMA–, ANA/ASMA+, PBC histology (immune cholangiopathy)

AIH–PSC Overlap

  • Positive ANA or ASMA
  • Radiographic PSC findings
  • Treatment: steroids + azathioprine + UDCA

Paris Criteria (AIH–PBC Overlap)

Sensitivity 92%, Specificity 97% — requires 2 of 3 criteria from each domain.
