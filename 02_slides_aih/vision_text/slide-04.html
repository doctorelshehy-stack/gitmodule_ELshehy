Autoimmune Hepatitis — Essentials

Essentials for Diagnosis

  • ▸Presence of autoantibodies & elevated serum IgG
  • ▸More common in females
  • ▸Abnormal liver biochemical tests
  • ▸Autoantibodies, elevated IgG, histologic findings
  • ▸Rule out viral, metabolic, inherited & drug-induced liver disease
  • ▸Overlap syndromes: PBC, PSC
  • ▸% have cirrhosis at presentation
  • ▸year mortality: 58%

Types of AIH

Type | Antibody

Type 1 (Classic) | ANA & ASMA >1:320 (anti-actin)

Type 2 | Anti-LKM antibodies

Type 3 | Anti-soluble liver antigen (ASLA)
