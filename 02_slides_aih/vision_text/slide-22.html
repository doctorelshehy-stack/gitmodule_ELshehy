Key Takeaways

  • ▸AIH**: Autoantibodies, elevated IgG, interface hepatitis — treat with steroids + immunomodulators
  • ▸PBC**: AMA-positive, cholestatic pattern, UDCA therapy — T-cell mediated bile duct destruction
  • ▸PSC**: Multifocal bile duct strictures, IBD association, 10–15% cholangiocarcinoma risk
  • ▸Overlap Syndromes**: AIH–PBC (Paris criteria) and AIH–PSC require combined treatment
  • ▸Vascular**: Budd-Chiari (TIPSS), VOD (defibrotide), PVT (anticoagulation) — always suspect in atypical presentations
