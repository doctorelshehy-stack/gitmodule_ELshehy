Prognosis & Key Outcomes

Autoimmune Hepatitis

  • 60–85% achieve remission
  • Normalization of enzymes & IgG
  • Remission at 18–24 months
  • 80% respond to steroids
  • 10% fail → ESLD
  • 40% have cirrhosis at presentation
  • 5-year mortality: 58%
  • 3% present with acute liver failure
  • 40% untreated die within 6 months

Primary Biliary Cirrhosis

  • Progressive disease
  • UDCA is main treatment
  • May progress to cirrhosis & ESLD
  • Most patients initially asymptomatic
  • Eventually develop fatigue & pruritus
  • AMA 95% sensitive

Primary Sclerosing Cholangitis

  • Progressive → secondary biliary cirrhosis
  • Portal HTN, varices, ascites
  • 10–15% develop cholangiocarcinoma
  • 70–90% have IBD
  • Small duct PSC may progress to classic PSC
  • LT may be needed in ESLD
