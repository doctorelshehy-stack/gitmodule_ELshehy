AIH — Treatment

Induction of Remission

First-line: Corticosteroids (Prednisone 40–60 mg with tapering)

Alternative: Budesonide 3 mg 2–3× daily — high hepatic first-pass clearance, minimal systemic side effects

Maintenance of Remission

Immunomodulating agents:

  • Azathioprine 50–100 mg/day (1–2 mg/kg/day)
  • Mycophenolate mofetil 1 g twice daily
  • Cyclosporine 3–5 mg/kg/day
  • Cyclophosphamide 1–1.5 mg/kg/day
  • Tacrolimus, Methotrexate

Common Agents & Adverse Effects

Agent Adverse Effects
Prednisone Cushingoid, DM, Hypertension, Osteopenia
Budesonide Minimal systemic side effects
Azathioprine Leukopenia, Drug-induced hepatitis, Pancreatitis, GI upset
Mycophenolate Leukopenia, Diarrhea, Nausea
Cyclosporine Nephrotoxic, Hypertension
Cyclophosphamide Pancytopenia, Cystitis
