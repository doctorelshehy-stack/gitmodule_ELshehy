Risk Factors for Cholesterol Gallstones

13 Known Risk Factors
01
Age > 40 years
02
Female sex (2× risk than men)
03
Oral contraception
04
High fat, low fiber diet
05
Obesity
06
Pregnancy (increased in multipara)
07
Hyperlipidaemia / Anti-hyperlipidaemic drugs (clofibrate)
08
Bile salt loss (ileal disease or resection)
09
Cystic fibrosis
10
Gall bladder dysmotility
11
Prolonged fasting / TPN
12
Liver cirrhosis
13
Biliary sludge
