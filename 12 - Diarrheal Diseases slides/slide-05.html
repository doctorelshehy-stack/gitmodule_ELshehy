Clinical Clues: Infectious vs Non-Infectious

Infectious

  • Fever
  • Pus and blood in stool
  • Epidemic
  • Travel
    • Bacterial: Symptoms onset WHILE IN visited country
    • Parasitic: Symptoms onset AFTER RETURN

Non-Infectious

  • Afebrile
  • Non-pus stool, Non-bloody
  • Sporadic
  • No travel

Key: Fever + blood/pus + epidemic + travel → Infectious | Afebrile + non-bloody + sporadic + no travel → Non-infectious
