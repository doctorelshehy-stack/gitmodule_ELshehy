9. AIDS-Associated Diarrhea

Causes of HIV-Associated Diarrhea

Acute or chronic diarrhea is frequent & may be caused by:

  1. Direct effects of HIV → incompletely understood mechanisms
  2. Medications → nelfinavir, didanosine or antibiotics
  3. Common sources occurring in the immunocompetent (CD4+ >200/mL), such as viral gastroenteritis or IBS
  4. Opportunistic infections

Opportunistic Infections by CD4+ Count

CD4+ < 200/mL

C. difficile, salmonellosis, viral gastroenteritis

CD4+ < 100/mL

Cryptosporidium, Microsporidia, Isospora belli

CD4+ < 50/mL

Mycobacterium avium, Cytomegalovirus
