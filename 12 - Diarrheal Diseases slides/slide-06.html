Acute Viral Gastroenteritis

Self-limited Viral Causes

Commonly due to:

  1. Norovirus
  2. Rotavirus
  3. Adenovirus
  4. Astrovirus

Rotavirus — Key Details

  • Mostly during winter months
  • Primary infection in infancy → moderate to severe illness
  • In adolescent → mild illness

Pathology:

Invade upper small intestine causing; villous damage, secondary transient disaccharide deficiency.

Clinical Picture:

Vomiting: 3-4 days, diarrhea: 7-10 days

Treatment:

Supportive + Addition of probiotic (lactobacillus GG) shorten duration of illness.
