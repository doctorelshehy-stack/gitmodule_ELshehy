HPS — Diagnostic Algorithm

Chronic Liver Disease
Pulse Oximetry (SaO₂)

SaO₂ ≥ 96%

No severe gas exchange abnormality; moderate to severe HPS unlikely

SaO₂ < 96%

Proceed to contrast echocardiography

Contrast-Enhanced Echocardiography

IPVD Negative

No HPS — consider other causes

IPVD Positive

Proceed to ABG analysis

Arterial Blood Gas Analysis

AaPO₂ < 15 mmHg

No HPS, solely IPVD. Follow-up on regular basis.

AaPO₂ ≥ 15, PaO₂ ≥ 80

Mild HPS — follow-up regularly

AaPO₂ ≥ 15, PaO₂ < 80

Proceed to PFT & Chest Imaging

PaO₂ < 60 mmHg

Severe HPS. Initiate LTOT (Long-Term Oxygen Therapy) and evaluate for LTX (Liver Transplantation) due to HPS.

PaO₂ ≥ 60 mmHg

Moderate HPS. Follow up on regular basis. Monitor for progression.
