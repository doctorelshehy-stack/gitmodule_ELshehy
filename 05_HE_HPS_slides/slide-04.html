HE — Definition

Definition: Hepatic encephalopathy (HE) is a chronically incapacitating syndrome of neuropsychiatric symptoms, which can develop in patients with both acute and chronic liver dysfunction once other known brain diseases have been excluded.

Key Concept: HE represents a spectrum of neuropsychiatric abnormalities ranging from subtle cognitive deficits to coma, occurring in the setting of liver insufficiency and/or porto-systemic shunting.
